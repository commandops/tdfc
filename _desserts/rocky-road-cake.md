Combine and cool: 

1 1/2 cup boiling water  
1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda  
1 cup chopped dates  

Mix in order given:

3/4 cup shortening  
1 cup sugar  
2 eggs  
1 3/4 cup flour  
3/4 teaspoon baking soda  
1/2 teaspoon salt  

Add date mixture and pour into greased 9x13 pan. Cover top with 1 cup of chocolate chips. 

Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
